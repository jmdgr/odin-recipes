Burger

Not only am I grill-pilled, but I am also chill-pilled with my delicios hamburgers - One can burger it up if one desires:

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make Patties.
  2. Heat up skillet or grill.
  3. Cook patty to desired done-ness. Cheese it.
  4. Let cool, and serve on a bun!
